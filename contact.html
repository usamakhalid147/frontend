The Functional Genetics and Functional Cancer Genomics groups at QIMR Berghofer are dedicated to the discovery and charactisation of cancer-related long noncoding RNAs (lncRNAs). We integrate genomics, bioinformatics and molecular biology approaches to elucidate lncRNA expression patterns, regulatory mechanisms and molecular functions relevant to cancer. We also assess how inherited variants identified through genome wide association studies (GWAS) and mutations identified through whole gene sequencing (WGS) alter lncRNA expression or function to promote cancer development. The ultimate aim is to pinpoint the key lncRNAs implicated in the development of cancer to identify new therapeutic opportunities.
